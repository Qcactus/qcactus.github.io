Qi Wan

Hi! I’m Qi Wan (万琪), a first year master’s student at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Prof. Xiangnan He and Prof. Xiang Wang. Previously, I studied at School of the Gifted Young, USTC, where I received a bachelor degree in Computer Science.

My research interest mainly lies in graph neural networks and information retrieval with applications in molecules, recommendations and social networks. Currently, I am working on developing expressive and reliable models for molecule representation learning.

Publication

January, 2022 In WWW 2022
Cross Pairwise Ranking for Unbiased Item Recommendation

We develop a new learning paradigm named Cross Pairwise Ranking (CPR) to alleviate the popularity bias in recommender systems. It achieves unbiased recommendation without knowing the exposure mechanism.
